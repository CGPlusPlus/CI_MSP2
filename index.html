Premier League Flip

A Premier League inspired spin on the classic family game Concentration

Start Game

How To Play

  • To start the game, click on any card
  • Clicking on cards will flip them over, to reveal a Teams crest
  • The aim of the game is to match 2 of the same crest by flipping them over consecutively
  • Selecting 2 Unmatched crests will flip them back over, testing the Players memory
  • You have 60 seconds to match all Team crests; doing so in as little moves as possible will gain you a better star rating.
  • Clicking reset will restart your game
